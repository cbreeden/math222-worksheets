Find $\v{a}^{\ll}$ and $\v{a}^{\perp}$ such that $\v{a} = \v{a}^{\ll} + \v{a}^{\perp}$,
where $\v{a}^{\ll}$ is parallel to $\v{b}$ and $\v{a}^{\perp}$ is perpendicular to $\v{b}$.
